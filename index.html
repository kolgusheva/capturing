About the Conference

Amid the decline in trade with Russia, the need for diversifying export destinations has become a priority for Ukrainian business. The Capturing New MarketsThe Capturing New Markets conference will:

  • explore Ukraine's export potential in EU, Turkey, and MENA markets,
  • provide practical knowledge on how to enter the new markets,
  • link potential business partners,
  • enable discussion on how to boost Ukraine's exports at the decision-making level.

Key questions for discussion

Defining Ukraine’s export potential

  • sectors: agriculture and food, chemicals, mining, machinery, pharmaceuticals, IT
  • thinking ahead: moving up the value chain
  • small and medium enterprises vs. big business

What are the main challenges for Ukrainian exporters and how to tackle them?

  • export financing, competing with local producers
  • thinking ahead: moving up the value chain

How to enter the new markets?

You will learn about:

  • legislation and certification
  • logistics and infrastructure
  • finding local partners
  • mentality and cultural peculiarities
  • approaching the customer: marketing strategy and distribution channels
