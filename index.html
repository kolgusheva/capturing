About the event

Amid declining dependence on trade with Russia, the need for diversifying export destinations has become a priority for Ukrainian business.

The Capturing New Markets conference seeks to:

  • – explore Ukraine's export potential in EU, Turkey, and MENA markets
  • – provide practical knowledge on how to enter these markets and link potential business partners
  • – provide a forum for how to boost Ukraine's exports at the decision-making level
Key topics for discussion

Defining Ukraine’s export potential. Which sectors and companies can compete?

What are the main challenges for Ukrainian exporters?

How to enter the new markets?

  • - legislation and certification process
  • - logistics and infrastructure
  • - cultural peculiarities and local business customs
  • - approaching the customer: marketing strategy and distribution channels
